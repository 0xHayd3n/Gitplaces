How should the repo overlay fill the screen?

When you click a repo card on Discover, how much of the screen should the overlay take up?

Full-screen overlay

Covers the entire window — identical to the current RepoDetail page, but no navigation. Feels immersive. Close button / Escape dismisses it.

Centered modal (~80% width/height)

Large dialog floating above Discover with a dimmed backdrop. The grid peeks around the edges. Click backdrop or Escape closes it.

Slide-in side panel (~55% width)

Panel slides in from the right, covering about half the screen. Discover grid stays partially visible on the left. Feels like a detail drawer.
